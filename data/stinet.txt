Links to Selected STINET Reports

The following is a listing of 120 reports relating to rocketry and space technology. This listing is somewhat arbitrary in the sense that it reflects my own particular interests. I've only had the opportunity to read a few of these articles, to date, and the choices were based largely on individual title and/or abstract. There are undoubtedly many more articles worthy of consideration, as the STINET collection contains around a million documents! All reports shown here are available for downloading in PDF format by clicking on the links. File sizes range from a few hundred kilobytes to tens of megabytes. Exceptionally large files are flagged as such.

Public STINET (Scientific & Technical Information Network) is available to the general public, free of charge. It provides access to citations of unclassified unlimited documents that have been entered into DTIC's Technical Reports Collection, as well as the electronic full-text of many of these documents.
